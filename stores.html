    Along with the Store discovery project, we worked on the Store profile to help people discover and visit interesting stores.

    Objective:

    • One of our core value lies in the discount we offer, so make sure its intuitive.
    • Design a scalable system that can support various content so it can scale.
    • Make finding stores easy.
    • Provide informations that can help people find out about the stores they don't know about.

    Exploration

    As our aim is to provide better deal and experience for our users, our target audiences were the people who cared about these offers and deals. With having specific target audience and our objective through the product we began considering many things. What is the most important things to show? what information do we need? where should the Map and photos be shown? or should they even have a map?. Here is some of the high fidelity exploration we made through many critiques.

    Outcome

    With the store profile we wanted to get people to find interesting stores where you can use Origami to get the discounts and being able to visit stores easily. To do this we surfaced the 3 most important elements: Map, Discount and Photos.
