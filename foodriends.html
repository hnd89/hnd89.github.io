    Foodriends is a fun little concept app that connects people with similar food tastes among local areas. Whether you are looking for people to go grab some food/coffee with or make new friends in new place through similar food interest this app will help you connect with people arounds you in more casual and fun way. While you can connect with people through similar food interests, it also provides optimised recommendation based on the places you liked and as a result you can discover a lot more places you will love.

    I come up with this idea from Tabelog app which has so much useful local restaurant data and where people want to go and went. It would be so much more useful if it can be used for the recommendation while it can also be used to connect people through their respective interest.

    Objective:

    • Connect people with local places and people
    • Discover more favorite places that people couldn't have come across

    Outcome

    Browsing

    You are looking for a nice places to eat or have a cup of tea. Here you can simply "favorite" or "skip" to save the place or see next potential place. The more you discover your favorite places, you will get optimised recommendation through this discovery experience. It enables people to discover not only the places you specifically looked for, but more places thats related to your own preference.

    Match

    When you have more than 10 common favorite places with other persons, you will be notified that you have matched with people with similar taste. It enables you to connect with people around your local areas whether you wish to go hang around or just to have a quick recommendations from them. You can also view their favorite lists to discover alot more which is based on similar interests.

    Profile

    If you are serous about connecting with your commons, you can view more of their profile and photos to learn what kind of person he/she is. Send some direct message to become friends and go out for a good times!

    My Profile

    To get people to know more about yourself, you can insert you introductions and photos here. You can also see all of your favorite places in this page.

    Settings

    Since you will expose yourself to other people through this platform, what people see and what/who you want to see should be under control. Here you can also filter price range and distance from your current location to match your better preferences.
