    Condé Nast UK GQ Competition・2013
    [Finalist Award]

    Task is to devise an alternative approach based on the same concept of MYGQ in order to improve user experience of saving and sharing feature within GQ ipad app.

    The concept of this GQ app is to create exclusive user experience. The foreground of this is to take you beyond magazine and bring trendy articles and videos focusing on your interest, which is picked based on categories from curated pages within your GQ content. The core value of this concept is to take you beyond curation and discovering more things that truly matters and tailoring GQ experience just for you.
